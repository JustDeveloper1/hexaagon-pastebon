Thank you for using Pastebon, an open-source Pastebin alternative. Your privacy is important to us, and we aim to keep this policy transparent and straightforward.

### **What Information We Collect**

We do not collect, store, or process any user data, such as IP addresses, ISPs, or browser information. Pastebon is designed to respect your privacy and ensure anonymity.

### **Third-Party Services**

Pastebon uses third-party services such as:

- **Supabase** (https://supabase.com): We use Supabase for user data management and storage. [Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Vercel** (https://vercel.com): We use Vercel as a server to store the website you are currently visiting and to process requests from visitor browsers. [Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Cloudflare**: (https://www.cloudflare.com): We use Cloudflare as an additional security system when you visit our website and to prevent unauthorized users from accessing our website. [Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy).

These services may collect information about you, such as your IP address or usage data, as outlined in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies to understand how they handle your data.

### **Cookies and Tracking**

Pastebon does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms. However, the third-party services mentioned above may use cookies or similar technologies.

### **Security**

While we strive to maintain a secure platform, we rely on third-party providers to safeguard the infrastructure of Pastebon. Please review their security practices for more details.

### **Changes to This Policy**

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to our services or third-party dependencies. The latest version will always be available on [our GitHub repository](https://github.com/hexaaagon/pastebon).

### **Use of Information**

By visiting and using Pastebon, you agree to our Privacy Policy and [Terms of Service](/docs/terms).
